Why repotrans ?

Repotrans,which means reports and transactions is an accounting system and a point of sale all in one to manage your business.
By using this system you get access to:
1.Daily and monthly sales or payment reports
2.Daily and monthly purchases reports
3.Daily and monthly expenses reports
4.Daily autogenerated income statment
5.An always upto date income statment
6.Compare your income across different periods
7.No need of expensive point of sales machines use your phone,tablet or pc to install the system
8.Digital receipts to track sales with printing option
9.An invoicing section to invoice your clients
10.No confusing pricing plans just subscribe for 450ksh or 4.5$ monthly ,and access all the features

For installation ,just open in chrome and you will prompted to add the system to home screen.
